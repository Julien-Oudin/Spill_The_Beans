Simple Risotto

40min - 480 kcal/per person

difficulty

price

  • 50g Arborio rice

  • small onion, finely chopped

  • cloves garlic, minced

  • 00ml vegetable broth (hot)

  • 0g grated Parmesan cheese

  • tbsp butter

  • tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic and Arborio rice. Cook for 1–2 minutes until rice edges look slightly translucent.
  4. Add a ladle of hot vegetable broth and stir until absorbed.
  5. Continue adding broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed before adding the next.
  6. Repeat for about 18–20 minutes, until rice is creamy and tender with a slight bite.
  7. Turn off the heat and stir in grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Let rest for 1 minute, then serve warm with optional garnish (extra cheese or herbs).
  9. Enjoy!
