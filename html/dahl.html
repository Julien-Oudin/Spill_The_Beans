Simple Lentil Dahl

35min - 390 kcal/per person

dishes

difficulty

price

  • 50g red lentils

  • onion, chopped

  • cloves garlic, minced

  • tsp fresh grated ginger

  • tomato, diced

  • 00ml coconut milk

  • tsp cumin

  • tsp turmeric

  • tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  1. Rinse red lentils under cold water until water runs clear. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onion until soft and golden.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, cumin, turmeric, and chili flakes. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add diced tomato and cook for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  5. Stir in the lentils, coconut milk, and 400ml of water. Bring to a gentle boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for about 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Once lentils are soft and creamy, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve warm with rice, naan, or as is. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.
  9. Enjoy!
