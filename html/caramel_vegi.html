Caramelized Vegi

60min - 550 kcal/per person

  • 00g Tofu

  • 00g Carrots

  •  Sugar

  •  Fish sauce

  •  Oyster sauce

  • tsp rice vinegar

  • tsp cumin
  • Salt
  1. Thinly slice the pork and mix it in a bowl with soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, pepper, and garlic. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the sugar with 1 tbsp of water over medium heat. Let it melt and turn amber
  3. Once it caramelizes, quickly add the marinated pork
  4. Sear and coat the pork in the caramel. Cook until it’s browned and caramelized, about 5–7 minutes.
  5. Push the pork to the side. Add a bit more oil if needed.
  6. Stir-fry the onion, carrots, and broccoli first
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning — more soy, sugar, or vinegar to balance sweet/savory/sour
  8. Spoon over steamed rice
  9. Enjoy !
