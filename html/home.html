Looking for easy, delicious vegetarian meals that don’t break the bank? You’ve come to the right place. At Spill the Beans, we share fresh, plant-based recipes that are simple to make, full of flavor, and made with ingredients you can actually find. Whether you’re a student, a beginner in the kitchen, or just curious about eating less meat, there’s something here for you.

Our goal is to prove that vegetarian cooking isn’t boring, complicated, or expensive. From hearty mains to quick lunches and tasty snacks, everything we post is tested by real students – us! We focus on affordable ingredients, straightforward steps, and flavors that even meat-lovers will enjoy.

This project was born out of a web development course at EFREI Paris, but it’s grown into something more: a fun, creative space where coding meets cooking, and where we share our passion for food with you. So grab a fork (or a spatula), dive into our recipes, and let’s spill the beans – together!
