About us

Welcome to Spill the Beans – a student-made hub for tasty, easy, and fully vegetarian recipes!

We’re two computer science students from EFREI Paris, and this site started as a simple web project. But since we both love food (and coding), it quickly turned into something more: a space where we share our favorite plant-based dishes with the world. All our recipes are tested in real student kitchens – meaning they’re affordable, simple to make, and full of flavor. Whether you’re vegetarian, curious, or just hungry, we hope you’ll find something you love here.

This project is about more than just food – it’s about creativity, sustainability, and proving that veggies can be the main event.

Thanks for stopping by and enjoy the recipes!

– The Spill the Beans 🌱
