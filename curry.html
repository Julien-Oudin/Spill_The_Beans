  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear, then cook it in boiling salted water according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes). Once cooked, drain and keep warm.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the finely chopped shallot and minced garlic, saut√©ing for 2-3 minutes until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped small tomato to the pan and cook for another 2 minutes until it softens and releases its juices.
  4. Sprinkle in the curry powder and stir well to coat the vegetables. Cook for 1-2 minutes to allow the spices to release their aroma.
  5. Peel and dice the sweet potato into small cubes. Add these to the pan along with the 200 ml of coconut milk. Stir to combine everything evenly.
  6. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, cover the pan with a lid, and cook for about 15 minutes, or until the sweet potato is tender when pierced with a fork. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. Season the curry with salt to taste. Stir gently to mix the herbs through the curry.
  8. Serve the hot curry over the cooked rice on plates, garnishing with coriander if desired. Enjoy your flavorful Indian Vegan Curry!
